Help & FAQs

Find answers to common questions and get help with any issues you may encounter.

How do I join the server?

To join the server, simply use the server address: surfnet.serveminecraft.net:25624 in your Minecraft client.

What are the server rules?

Please respect all players, no cheating or exploiting bugs. Full rules can be viewed in our Discord server.

How can I report a player?

You can report players by contacting an admin through Discord with the player's username and details of the incident.

Are there any plugins on the server?

Yes! We have a variety of plugins that enhance gameplay, providing unique features and functionalities.

How can I support the server?

You can support the server by purchasing items from our store or making a donation directly through our Discord.
